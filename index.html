About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you must battle against endless hordes of monsters. Armed with various weapons and abilities, your objective is simple: survive as long as possible against the relentless enemy waves.

As you progress through the game, you'll collect experience points to level up, unlock new abilities, and enhance your character's powers. Every run offers a unique experience with different power-ups and enemy combinations, making each playthrough a fresh challenge.

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Test your survival skills against increasingly difficult waves of monsters that never end. How long can you last?

Character Progression

Level up during each run to unlock new abilities and increase your power to face tougher enemies.

Various Weapons

Discover and upgrade different weapons with unique attack patterns and special abilities.

How To Play

Use your keyboard to move your character around the arena. Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies. Collect experience gems from defeated monsters to level up and choose new abilities. Survive as long as possible while facing increasingly difficult waves of monsters.

Strategy Tips

Focus on movement and positioning to avoid being surrounded. Prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle, whether that's high damage, area control, or defensive abilities. Don't forget to collect health power-ups when they appear during your run.
